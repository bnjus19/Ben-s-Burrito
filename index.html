Ben Njus

Ben's Burrito is conveniently located at the corner of Gerald Ave and Mesa Ave. Ben's burrito was started in 2026 by Ben Njus on a mission to make the freshest breakfast burritos in town. The tortillas are made from scratch, the eggs layed by cage free grown chickens, and lettuce and tomatoes grown hydroponically with filtered water and no pesticides. We use fresh cut Russert potatoes and fresh roasted green chile in our burritos.
